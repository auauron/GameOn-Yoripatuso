# Asset Drop Zones

The prototype runs without final assets. Replace placeholders by dropping team assets into these folders and assigning them in the Godot Inspector.

## Art

- `art/characters/` - player sprites and animations
- `art/artifacts/` - Eye Piece, Nose Piece, and artifact presentation art
- `art/environment/` - terrain, structures, props, foliage, and effects
- `art/ui/` - panels, buttons, icons, and typography assets

## Audio

- `audio/ambience/` - wind, water, settlement, and nature loops
- `audio/activity/` - pottery, goldworking, market, and interaction sounds
- `audio/music/` - exploration and discovery music
- `audio/voice/` - historically reviewed narration, chants, or dialect recordings

Keep source files outside the project when possible. Add only game-ready exports here.
